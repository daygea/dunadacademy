How to enroll in the Dunad Academy

  • Begin Your Application

    Prior to commencing your application, it's crucial to establish a strong understanding of the learning path you've chosen, whether it's Software Development, Data Science or UI/UX Design.   • Start Learning Courses

    To better prepare for Academy Training, we recommend completing a series of online courses. This preparatory step aims to strengthen your skillset and greatly increases your chances of securing a place in the training program.

  • Take on the Challenge

    After finishing the courses, you'll progress to the next stage: the challenge. This is a crucial opportunity to showcase the knowledge and skills you've developed, serving as a concrete demonstration of your readiness for the Academy Training.

Dunad Academy is committed to discovering and fostering young talent, providing avenues to employment opportunities across the wider tech landscape. The initiative is crafted to equip participants with vital leadership and technical proficiencies, guaranteeing their realization of maximum potential. Through targeted, practical training customized for each educational track, the Academy pledges to deliver a rewarding journey that establishes the groundwork for success in the technology realm.

To qualify for the Academy Training, applicants must:

Demonstrate a strong foundational understanding in their selected learning path.

Possess the essential tools necessary for effective participation in a remote learning setting, including a dependable laptop, internet connectivity, and access to electricity.

The Academy Training spans a duration of 12 months.

Because of the dedication demanded by the Academy Training, participants are anticipated to be fully accessible throughout the program, which could pose challenges for full-time university students.

The credentials of participants will be stored in our database. Our network of partnered companies may provide employment opportunities based on our referrals.
